"As a regular guy I want to track my expenses"

task


Stories

1. As a guy I want to be able to easily create/modify/delete an expense.

2. As a guy I want to have an overview/list of my all expenses with total costs at the bottom.

3. As a guy I want to be able to easily mark expense as paid/unpaid from the list.
